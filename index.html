The Project

Just Jammin' is inspired by a lot of open source project out there which help us in doing the work we like to do, follow our passion that too without taking any amount of money from us. The aim is to make this project so big that most of the people interested in music should become happy and satisfied after using Just Jammin'. The project is very new and not yet upto the mark, we would aprreciate inputs from you all both as a user as well as a developer or music expert or design expert. There have been many DAWs out there many are free but a lot are paid too and the thing I observed was that the free ones are not that good as the paid are. For eg. FL studios is one of the best DAW out in the market but it is paid it is not that costly but still the common people who do jobs or are students are not that much dedicated to buy a software and work on it, so we need these type of open sources and free software. There has been a misconception that free or open sources dont provide that much as their paid alternatives provide and this misconsumption has been successfully bashed by the great open sources like blender and we like to grow like them. Virtual Instruments

Click on any Instrument and Start Jammin'
At the moment there are less Instruments with not all the note options, but we look forward to add more and more instruments along with sequnecers, synth rack, piano roll, audio effects and lot many more stuff. If you wish you can help us for the same.

Drumkit

This Drumkit includes almost all basic parts of a concert type drumkit.Enjoy playing it, so do learn some new stuff start your screen recording and refer to it again after some time.

Piano

This is a basic 12 key piano which will introduce you to thee most basic and must learn instrument for music makers. Learn, Play, Enjoy and Record.

Guitar

Here is the basic outlay of guitar with only six notes to play. The instrument is under work, we will be adding all the notes af a basic guitar as soon as possible.

Music Box

Many of us just like music, we dont really know the music theory nor we do know to play any instrument. So, this is a instrument for people like you. Just take your mouse and tap on tiles and make music. So Simple.
